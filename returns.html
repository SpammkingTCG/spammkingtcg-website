Customer Support

Returns & Refunds.

Customer guidance for returns, damaged items and order issues.

Returns Guidance

Clear, fair and collector-aware.

01

Returns overview

Return options will depend on the product, selling platform and condition of the item. Website checkout terms will be finalised before launch.

DMG

Damaged items

If an order arrives damaged, keep the packaging and contact SpammKing TCG with photos and order details as soon as possible.

ERR

Incorrect items

If the wrong item is sent, contact SpammKing TCG with the order details so the issue can be reviewed and corrected.

SEAL

Sealed products

Sealed products should usually remain unopened if a return is being requested. Opened products may need separate review.

CARD

Singles and graded cards

Singles and graded cards depend heavily on condition. Product listings should clearly describe condition before purchase.

EM

How to contact us

Email hello@spammkingtcg.co.uk with the product name, order reference and a short explanation.

If an order was placed through eBay, the eBay order process and buyer support flow may also apply.
