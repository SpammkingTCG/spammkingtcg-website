Cookies and Storage

What the site currently uses.

ESS

Essential cookies and storage

The site may use basic browser storage for features needed by the current experience, such as saving wishlist items locally.

WL

Wishlist localStorage

Wishlist and recently viewed products are stored in your browser using localStorage. This stays on your device and can be cleared in your browser settings.

AN

Analytics cookies

Analytics are not required for the current static store experience. If analytics are added later, this policy and any consent approach should be updated.

3P

Third-party links

Links to eBay and other external sites may use their own cookies or tracking. Those services are controlled by the third party, not this website.

CTRL

Managing cookies

You can manage or clear cookies and localStorage through your browser settings. Clearing storage may remove saved wishlist or recently viewed products.

NO

No banner for now

A cookie banner has not been added because the current site does not intentionally use non-essential analytics or advertising cookies.
