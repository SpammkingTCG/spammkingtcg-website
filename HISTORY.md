# The Story of SpammKing TCG

*"Every great business starts with a single idea."*

---

# Before SpammKing TCG

Like many collectors, it all started with opening packs.

Collecting quickly became more than a hobby.

It became something to learn about.

Understand.

Share.

And eventually build into something bigger.

Rather than simply collecting cards, we became interested in grading, investing, sealed products and helping other collectors find products they genuinely wanted.

---

# The First Sales

The journey began by selling on eBay.

Starting small.

Learning how to:

- Package safely.
- Photograph products.
- Communicate with buyers.
- Build feedback.
- Understand market prices.
- Learn which products collectors really wanted.

Every sale helped build experience.

Every positive feedback built trust.

---

# Building a Brand

Eventually it became clear that simply selling on eBay wasn't enough.

A real brand needed:

- A name.
- A logo.
- A website.
- A vision.

SpammKing TCG was created with one simple goal:

To build a business collectors could genuinely trust.

---

# Becoming a Business

The next milestone was registering as a UK Sole Trader.

This transformed the project from a hobby into a real business.

From this point onwards the focus became long-term growth rather than simply buying and selling cards.

---

# Building the Website

The website began as a simple landing page.

Over time it evolved into a premium collector-focused website inspired by modern technology brands.

Rather than copying other TCG stores, the aim was to create something cleaner, simpler and more premium.

Every section has been carefully planned with future expansion in mind.

---

# Learning the Industry

Building a trading card business means constantly learning.

This includes understanding:

- Distribution
- Wholesale pricing
- VAT
- Grading
- Market trends
- Customer service
- Website development
- Branding
- SEO
- Inventory management

Every conversation, every mistake and every success has contributed to making the business stronger.

---

# The Search for Distributors

One of the biggest challenges has been gaining access to official distributors.

Applications have been made across the UK and Europe while building relationships within the hobby.

This process has reinforced an important lesson:

Great businesses are built through patience and persistence.

---
