# SpammKing TCG Vision

*"Built by Collectors, for Collectors."*

---

# Our Mission

SpammKing TCG exists to create one of the United Kingdom's most trusted destinations for Trading Card Games.

Our goal is simple:

Provide collectors with genuine products, fair pricing, excellent customer service and a premium buying experience that we would want ourselves.

Every order should feel like it has been packed by another collector—not a warehouse.

---

# Our Vision

To become one of the UK's leading independent Trading Card retailers.

We want SpammKing TCG to grow from a small online business into a recognised and respected brand within the collecting community.

Long-term, we aim to operate:

- A premium online store
- A thriving collector community
- A physical retail store
- A trusted destination for collectors across the UK

We are building for decades, not months.

---

# Our Values

## Trust

Trust is earned.

We believe customers should never have to question:

- Authenticity
- Condition
- Communication
- Delivery
- Customer Service

Every decision should build trust.

---

## Collectors First

We're collectors ourselves.

Every product is handled with the same care we expect when buying for our own collections.

Our customers are collectors first, customers second.

---

## Quality

We prefer quality over quantity.

Rather than listing thousands of products, we'd rather carefully choose products collectors genuinely want.

Premium service matters more than having the biggest catalogue.

---

## Honesty

We will always communicate honestly.

If something is delayed, we'll say so.

If something isn't perfect, we'll explain why.

We believe transparency creates long-term customers.

---

## Community

Trading cards are more than products.

They create friendships.

Communities.

Stories.

Memories.

We want SpammKing TCG to become part of that community.

# Business Philosophy

We are not trying to become the cheapest retailer.

We are trying to become the retailer customers trust the most.

If customers trust us, they will return.

If they return, the business grows.

Long-term relationships matter more than short-term profits.
