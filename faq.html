FAQ

Common questions.

Are your products genuine?

Yes. SpammKing TCG is being built around genuine products, trusted sourcing and collector confidence.

Do you sell sealed products?

Yes. The catalogue supports sealed products such as booster boxes, Elite Trainer Boxes, starter decks, bundles and accessories.

Do you sell singles?

Singles are part of the roadmap and product system. Listings should clearly show condition before purchase.

Do you sell graded cards?

Yes, graded cards are supported in the product catalogue for future collector vault listings.

How quickly do you dispatch?

Dispatch details will be confirmed before full website checkout launches. eBay orders should follow the dispatch information shown on eBay.

Do you accept returns?

Returns guidance is being prepared for launch. See the draft Returns & Refunds page for current wording.

Do you ship outside the UK?

The business is UK-based. International shipping options have not been confirmed for the website yet.

How are cards packaged?

Cards and sealed products should be packed with collector-standard protection suited to the product type.

Can I pre-order products?

Pre-orders are planned for the future store. Current release pages may show placeholder pre-order or register-interest statuses.

How do I contact you?

Email hello@spammkingtcg.co.uk or use the details on the Contact page.
