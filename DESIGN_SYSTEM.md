# SpammKing TCG Design System

---

# Brand Personality

SpammKing TCG should feel:

- Premium
- Modern
- Luxury
- Professional
- Minimal
- Trustworthy
- Collector Focused

Never:

- Childish
- Cheap
- Cluttered
- Overly colourful

# Typography

Primary Font:

Poppins

Headings:

700-800 weight

Large spacing.

Body:

400-500 weight.

Readable.

Generous line height.

---

# Buttons

Primary

Gold background

Rounded pill shape

Dark text

Hover:

Lift slightly

Soft glow

---

Secondary

Transparent

Light border

White text

Hover:

Dark glass background

Gold border

---

# Cards

Rounded corners.

Soft shadow.

Glass effect where appropriate.

Hover:

Small lift.

Shadow increase.

Subtle animation.

---

# Navigation

Sticky.

Glass background.

Blur effect.

Gold hover underline.

Always visible.

---

# Hero

Large typography.

Minimal text.

Strong call to action.

High contrast.

Large whitespace.

---

# Spacing

Large sections.

Generous margins.

Comfortable reading width.

Avoid cramped layouts.

---

# Animations

Duration:

200-400ms

Style:

Ease-out

Subtle only.

Never distracting.

---

# Icons

Use simple modern icons.

Outlined where possible.

Gold accents.

---

# Images

High quality.

Professional.

Consistent style.

Avoid clutter.

---

# Mobile

Desktop first.

Tablet second.

Mobile third.

Buttons:

Touch friendly.

No horizontal scrolling.

Readable typography.

---

# Accessibility

Maintain strong contrast.

Keyboard navigation.

Visible focus states.

Semantic HTML.

Alt text.

---

# Performance

Optimised images.

Lazy loading.

Minimal JavaScript.

Clean CSS.

---

# Future Design Goals

As the website grows:

Maintain visual consistency.

Every new page should immediately look like part of the same premium brand.

When in doubt:

Choose simplicity over complexity.

Quality over quantity.

User experience above everything else.
