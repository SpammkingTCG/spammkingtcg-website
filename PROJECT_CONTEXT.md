# SpammKing TCG - Project Context

## Overview

SpammKing TCG is an independent UK trading card retailer focused on providing collectors with genuine products, premium customer service and a modern online shopping experience.

The business is currently built around:

- Pokémon TCG
- One Piece Card Game
- Disney Lorcana
- Magic: The Gathering
- Yu-Gi-Oh!

Current sales are primarily through eBay while the website is being developed.

The long-term vision is to become one of the UK's leading independent trading card retailers before eventually opening a physical store.

---

# Brand Vision

The website should immediately communicate:

- Premium
- Professional
- Trustworthy
- Modern
- Collector focused

It should never feel childish or aimed only at children.

The website should feel closer to premium technology brands than traditional card shops.

Inspirations include:

- Apple
- Nothing
- Tesla
- Razer
- Porsche
- Modern luxury e-commerce

# Business Goals

Create a website that inspires trust before customers ever purchase.

The website should look like a premium retailer regardless of current business size.

The design should scale naturally into a full e-commerce platform.

---

# Customer Experience

Visitors should immediately feel that they are buying from collectors who genuinely care about:

- Authenticity
- Customer service
- Safe packaging
- Fast dispatch
- Community
- Long-term trust

---

# Development Philosophy

Every change should improve one or more of:

- User Experience
- Accessibility
- Performance
- Responsiveness
- Maintainability
- SEO

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

Build for long-term growth.

Quality over speed.
