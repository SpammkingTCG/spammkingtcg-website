Business Summary

Independent, collector-led and built for growth.

SpammKing TCG is a UK-based independent trading card retailer currently selling primarily through eBay while developing a premium direct website.

The brand is built around genuine products, careful packaging, clear communication and a collector-first customer experience.

The website already supports products, sets, releases, wishlist, support pages and an eBay-first buying journey.
