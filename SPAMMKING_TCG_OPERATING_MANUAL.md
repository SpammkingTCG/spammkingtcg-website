# SpammKing TCG Operating Manual

## Purpose

This manual documents how SpammKing TCG should operate as a business, not just as a website.

It is a living document. Update it as real suppliers, processes, stock rules and customer habits become clearer.

## Vision

Build SpammKing TCG into a trusted premium UK trading card retailer known for genuine products, careful packaging, collector-first service and a professional brand.

## Mission

Help collectors buy trading card products with confidence by offering honest product information, safe packaging, clear communication and a modern shopping experience.

## Values

- Authenticity
- Trust
- Careful packaging
- Clear communication
- Collector-first decisions
- Long-term brand building
- Professional presentation

## Stock Rules

Before buying stock, consider:

- Expected demand
- Current market price
- Fees
- Postage and packaging cost
- Storage space
- Risk of price drops
- Whether the product fits the brand

Product categories:

- Sealed products
- Singles
- Graded cards
- Accessories
- Preorder candidates

## Pricing Rules

Track:

- Buy price
- Platform fees
- Packaging cost
- Postage cost
- Target margin
- Competitor pricing
- Reinvestment amount

Avoid racing to the bottom. The brand should compete on trust, service and presentation, not only price.

## eBay Sales Process

Suggested process:

1. Photograph product clearly.
2. Check product condition.
3. Create accurate eBay listing.
4. Add product to website data if appropriate.
5. Pack to collector standard after sale.
6. Mark dispatched promptly.
7. Keep communication polite and clear.
8. Record feedback and repeated customer questions.

## Packaging Standard

Packaging should protect condition and create confidence.

For sealed products:

- Check seals and corners before packing.
- Use suitable box or mailer.
- Add padding to reduce movement.
- Avoid crushing pressure.

For singles:

- Sleeve
- Top loader or card saver
- Team bag
- Rigid mailer or protected envelope

For graded cards:

- Sleeve the slab where appropriate.
- Wrap securely.
- Use a sturdy box or protected mailer.

## Product Content Workflow

For each real product:

1. Confirm product name.
2. Confirm game, set and product type.
3. Add real price.
4. Add stock or honest availability state.
5. Add eBay URL if available.
6. Add real description.
7. Add image paths.
8. Check product page.
9. Check category and release pages.

## Feature Decision Rule

Before building a feature, ask:

- Does this help customers buy cards?
- Does this help suppliers trust us?
- Does this reduce business friction?

If not, wait.
